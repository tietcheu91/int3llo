Nos Partenaires

The rise of new technologies has been a turning point for the life style of the human being. The overwhelming majority of people welcome the announcement of new gadgets or features with a lot of excitement. These new tools have, for the most part, tremendously impacted the way we act, interact and communicate with people from different cultures and backgrounds. Even if everybody agrees that technology has a significant influence on our daily life, it is controversial as to whether it is a positive or negative influence. Nowadays, the excitement around the technological industry has calmed down. In fact, it appears very clear that new technologies have a more destructive and addictive image than a revolutionary means of communication as originally presented.
